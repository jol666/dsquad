Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Why PPF is a must-have:.

  • Self-healing – scratches disappear with heat
  • Impact-resistant – protects against chips
  • UV filtering – preserves paint color
  • Gloss or matte finish options
Borophene Coating (Exclusive)

What makes it extraordinary?.

  • Ultimate scratch and corrosion resistance
  • Superior adhesion and hydrophobic properties
  • Incredible thermal and chemical resistance

Graphene Coating

Why choose graphene?

  • Anti-static and dust-repellent properties
  • Extreme heat resistance (ideal for Indian summers)
  • Longer durability than ceramic coatings
  • Unbeatable slickness and gloss
Ceramic Coating

This invisible armor defends against:

  • UV rays that dull and fade paint over time
  • Bird droppings, tree sap, and acid rain
  • Light scratches and swirl marks
  • Hard water stains and chemical etching
